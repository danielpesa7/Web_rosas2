We are dedicated to growing and distributing carefully selected roses, of high quality and duration.

Living Flower Company

Amazing crops

Living Flower Company was born as a small Colombian company in a rose crop in the city of Cogua, Cundinamarca. Since 2016 we have invested in the business to guarantee the quality of each of our flowers, thus achieving the satisfaction of our customers.

Today our flowers reach the international market.

Mission

We are a family business passionate about providing moments of happiness to people who believe in the romanticism of flowers.
Therefore, our crops have high quality standards that allow us to participate in the world market.

Vision

We want to be a leading company in the floricultural sector, producing and exporting high quality Colombian flower
in company of a motivated human team that is proud of participating in this dream.
